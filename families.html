The Ravenscroft Family

Description: The central family, wealthy and feared, hosting the masquerade in their estate. The Ravenscrofts are obsessed with grandeur and reputation, but their wealth conceals dark secrets and mounting debts.

Family Members:

  • Lavinia Ravenscroft - Elegant hostess with hidden affections
  • Cornelius Ravenscroft - Stubborn patriarch with a fierce temper
  • Bartholomew Ravenscroft - Reckless, charming, financially ruined
  • Minerva Ravenscroft - Free-spirited sister who distrusts the family's rigidity

Family Connections:

With the Carroway Family: The families partnered on a mining venture that has recently declined due to economic issues and worker strikes. Maximilian and Cornelius have been seen arguing about the Carroways receiving less than their fair share of profits.

With the Ashworth Family: The Ashworths attend annual hunting parties with Cornelius, also including Algernon and Florence. The last hunting party included Lavinia and went awry. Silas' invitation was specially ordered by Cornelius, though even the Ashworth family is unsure why.

With the Whitcombe Family: Algernon and Cornelius share a friendship strengthened over many years. Nelle's direct invitation from Lavinia has cast confusion about the relationship. Nelle, Minerva, and Lavinia were seen discussing Silas.

With the Vale Family: Lavinia had a fond childhood with Karissa, however this fondness faded throughout the years. Cornelius and Florence remained close friends, and Cornelius had ties with Jasper related to Jasper's gambling adventures.

With the Blackwood Family: The Blackwood family are distant cousins of Lavinia, however Lavinia's success had caused a recent strain in the relationships they shared.

With the Moreau Family: Bartholomew was a frequent customer of Francesca's, indulging in the optional medicine that she provided. Cornelius was referred to her by Bartholomew for his medicine.

The Whitcombe Family

Description: Ambitious and calculating, with ties to business and society scandals. The Whitcombes are known for their peculiar interests in alchemy and their talent for gathering secrets.

Family Members:

  • Algernon Whitcombe - Alchemist and Scientific Researcher; respected in his community
  • Nelle Whitcombe - Quiet but calculating gossip collector

Connections to the Ravencrofts:

Algernon and Cornelius have maintained a strong friendship over many years, attending hunting parties together. Nelle's arrival and direct invitation from Lavinia has caused some confusion about the nature of their relationship. The Whitcombe women were recently seen discussing Silas with Minerva and Lavinia.

The Vale Family

Description: Wealthy and proud, the Vales are protective of their reputation at all costs. Behind their refined exterior lie gambling debts, forgeries, and forbidden passions.

Family Members:

  • Karissa Vale - Proud wife, fiercely protective of reputation
  • Jasper Vale - Charming gambler, hopelessly indebted
  • Florence Vale - Aspiring Artist and Dreamy Idealist

Connections to the Ravencrofts:

The Vale family has long-standing financial entanglements with the Ravencrofts, with Jasper owing considerable gambling debts to Cornelius. Kerensa and Lavinia were childhood friends before Lavinia's marriage, though their relationship has become strained over the years. Florence is a frequent visitor to the Ravenscroft estate, often seen in the gardens. Jasper and Bartholomew frequent the same gentleman's clubs, where Bartholomew allegedly encourages Jasper's reckless habits. Florence attends the annual hunting parties. The Vale family's attendance at the masquerade was considered essential by Cornelius, though the reasons remain unclear.

The Ashworth Family

Description: A stern family with powerful ties and a reputation for ruthlessness. The Ashworths are entangled in debts, secrets, and dangerous knowledge brought from distant travels.

Family Members:

  • Mortimer Ashworth - Stern patriarch with powerful connections
  • Donovan Ashworth - Entitled, reckless son, a Military Officer on leave.
  • Digory Ashworth - Idealistic son seeking recognition
  • Silas Ashworth - World-weary uncle to the Ashworth boys, and brother to Mortimer, with dangerous secrets

Connections to the Ravencrofts:

The Ashworth family notably attends annual hunting parties with Cornelius, which also include Algernon and Florence Vale. The last hunting party included Lavinia and went awry under mysterious circumstances. Even the Ashworth family is unsure of Silas' involvement and why he was invited - reportedly, this invitation was on special order by Cornelius himself.

The Blackwood Family

Description: Vain socialites consumed by jealousy and competition. The Blackwood sisters are known for their beauty, their rivalry, and their obsession with the tragic figure of Basil.

Family Members:

  • Honoria Blackwood - Socialite who is jealous of Lavinia's status
  • Selene Blackwood - Mystical, mysterious sister of Honoria and Vivienne
  • Vivienne Blackwood Carroway - Born Blackwood, married Maximilian Carroway to unite families

Connections to the Ravencrofts:

Honoria and Lavinia have been bitter social rivals since Lavinia's debut into society. The Blackwood family was once wealthier than the Ravencrofts, but recent reversals have shifted the balance of power. Selene briefly worked as a companion to Minerva before the arrangement ended under mysterious circumstances. There are whispered rumors that Cornelius once courted Honoria before choosing to marry into Lavinia's family for strategic reasons. The youngest Blackwood sister, Vivienne, married into the struggling Carroway family, creating a strategic alliance between the two houses. The Blackwoods' invitation to the masquerade surprised many in society, given the public tension between the families.

The Carroway Family

Description: Distant cousin fallen on hard times, desperate to restore their fortunes. The Carroways are willing to go to great lengths to restore their once great name

Family Members:

  • Maximilian Carroway - Distant cousin to Cornelius, fallen on hard times, married to Vivienne Blackwood

Connections to the Ravencrofts:

The Carroway family and the Ravenscroft family worked together on a special mining venture. Recent issues with the economy and worker strikes have resulted in a decline in profits from their joint venture. The Carroway family allegedly receives a less than equitable share of the profits, and Maximilian and Cornelius have been seen arguing heatedly about this matter. Maximilian's marriage to Vivienne Blackwood has created a strategic alliance between the Carroway and Blackwood families, providing additional leverage in business dealings.

The Moreau Family

Description: Mysterious and cunning, rumored to deal in potions and dark arts. The Moreaus possess dangerous knowledge, blackmail material, and a talent for manipulation.

Family Members:

  • Francesca Moreau - Herbalist and Potion dealer, holds recipes for exotic remedies.
  • Augustus Moreau - Intellectual scholar, teaching scientific studies at University
  • Anastasia Moreau - Investigative journalist, conducting expos√© on elite families

Connections to the Ravencrofts:

Francesca supplies various "remedies" and "tonics" to the Ravenscroft household, with Bartholomew being a frequent customer. Augustus served as a tutor to the younger Ravencrofts years ago, though he was dismissed after a disagreement with Cornelius. The Moreau family possesses certain knowledge about Ravenscroft family history that makes them both valuable and dangerous as allies. Anastasia has been seen at the Ravenscroft estate on several occasions prior to the party, supposedly conducting "research" for her father. Cornelius allegedly owes the Moreau family a significant favor from years past, which some speculate is why they received such a prominent invitation.
